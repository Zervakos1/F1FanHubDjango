About F1 Fan Hub

F1 Fan Hub helps fans organize race weekends, manage watch parties, and track personal plans with clear analytics.

The website demonstrates Django structure, database-backed features, modular JavaScript, reusable CSS, role-based dashboards, simulated purchases, and responsive design.
